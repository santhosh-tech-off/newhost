ADMISSION

"After complete / pass the qualifying examination (HSC for B.E / B.Tech First year, Diploma for B.E / B.Tech Second year (Lateral Entry) & UG degree for M.E / M.Tech) the candidate may register his / her name in respective branch of study based on his/ her interest. The candidate may register his / her name in respective branch of study / department in two different ways, 1. Direct mode and 2. Online mode In direct mode, the candidate visit our college campus and meet the admission authorities to clarifying his / her doubts related to admission (if any) before registration, and then pay the registration fee for his / her name registration. In Online mode, the candidate may contact our admission authorities through phone and ask his / her doubts related to admission (if any) before registration, and then pay the registration fee for his / her name registration".
